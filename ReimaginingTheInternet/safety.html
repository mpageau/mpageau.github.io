Safety

Safe-Swap puts your safety first.


To ensure your safety, when you shop with Safe-Swap your seller will place your items in one of the designated safe lockers at your local USPS.

Once your purchase is in its designated locker, you will recieve a text to the number linked to your account with specific instructions on how to retrieve your purchase.


Be sure to follow these tips when communicating with your seller:


  1. Never send money or personal information before seeing items
  2. Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, walk away
  3. If lockers are unavailable, use our safe meeting spots at local police stations
  4. Report suspicious activity immediately
