About Safe-Swap

Safe-Swap is a selling service designed to keep YOU safe.


Safe-Swap revolutionizes local buying and selling by eliminating the risks and inconveniences of traditional person-to-person transactions.

Our platform connects community members through a secure, contactless exchange system using strategically placed lockers at local post offices.


How We Work

We understand the challenges of online marketplaces: scheduling meetups, safety concerns, and the awkwardness of in-person exchanges.


Safe-Swap solves these problems by providing a simple, secure solution:


  1. List Your Item: Upload your item with details and set your price.
  2. Buyer Confirms: When someone purchases, they select a convenient post office locker location.
  3. Secure Drop-Off: You securely place your item in a designated locker.
  4. Easy Pickup: The buyer retrieves the item using a unique access code.

Our Commitment to Safety


  1. Zero In-Person Interactions: Completely eliminate face-to-face meetings.
  2. Verified Post Office Locations: Only use official, monitored postal facilities.
  3. Secure Locker System: Each transaction uses a unique, time-limited access code.
  4. Buyer and Seller Protection: Our platform ensures accountability for both parties.

Community-Focused


Safe-Swap isn't just a marketplace—it's a community solution. We're making local commerce safer, more convenient, and more accessible for everyone.
