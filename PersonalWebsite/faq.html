MORE ABOUT ME

Where are you from?
I was originally born in Detroit, MI, but moved to Roanoke Rapids, NC when I was 2-years old and have lived there ever since. Roanoke Rapids is a super tiny town in Northeastern North Carolina with only about 15,000 residents.
What are your post-grad plans?
Currently, my plan is to graduate in May and move to Washington, DC to begin a career in public relations. Although I don't have a job (yet), I am hopeful that the recruitment process will be on my side and I will be employed before summer :).
Favorite animal?
I am a HUGE animal lover and would love you answer this question by saying "all animals", but if I had to pick one it would definitely be otters. I am also a big fan of farm animals and frequent the Spring Haven Farm here in Chapel Hill to hold the baby goats.
Dream job?
In a perfect world, I would be a publicist at Vogue. This feels like such a basic answer, but it has been my dream ever since watching "The Devil Wears Prada" for the first time when I was 10-years old at a sleepover with some of my best friends.
If you had unlimited money, what would you do?
I would absolutely, 10000% travel to every country in Europe. Well probably all of the countries in the world since I would have unlimited money. Experiencing new cultures and going to new places is one of my favorite parts of traveling.
